## Why You're Not Getting Organic Follows Yet

Here is the honest breakdown:

**Gap 1 — No Thread Engine**
Single tweets get low reach. Threads (3-7 connected tweets) get 4-8x more impressions and profile visits. You have zero thread capability right now.

**Gap 2 — No Quote-Tweet Engine**
Quote-tweeting a viral post with a sharp take is one of the highest-conversion growth tactics on X. You don't do this at all. The VIP Sniper replies in a thread, but a QRT (Quote ReTweet) shows up on the original post AND in your own feed.

**Gap 3 — No Engagement Farming on Viral Posts**
When BTC or ETH makes a big move, there are thousands of posts getting massive engagement. Commenting on those with a sharp take (not generic) drives profile visits. You currently only reply to mentions (people who already know you), not to trending viral posts.

**Gap 4 — No Polling / Interactive Content**
X's algorithm gives massive reach boosts to polls. A simple "Which chain survives the 2026 bear?" style poll from your persona gets engagement from people who've never heard of you.

**Gap 5 — Posting Frequency + Timing**
8 posts/day is decent but the timing needs to hit X's peak windows. The algorithm rewards consistency at the right hours (8am EST, 12pm EST, 5pm EST, 9pm EST are peak crypto audience windows).

## The Full Automated Growth Plan

This plan adds 4 new automated behaviors that run without you ever touching anything.

### New Behavior 1: Viral Post Comment Engine (Highest Impact)
A new scheduled function that:
1. Uses Tavily to find the top 3-5 most-engaged crypto/AI posts in the last 6 hours
2. Filters for posts with 500+ likes (viral threshold)
3. Uses Claude to write a sharp, persona-consistent comment
4. Posts it as a reply to those viral posts
5. Rate limit: max 5 comments per day, 1 per run, to avoid spam signals

This is the single most powerful organic growth tactic — you appear in the notifications of people who already follow the viral account.

### New Behavior 2: Thread Engine (2x Reach Multiplier)
Replace 2 of your 8 daily single-tweet posts with threads:
- Thread structure: Hook tweet → 3-4 insight tweets → Closing tweet with a question
- The question at the end drives replies, which the algorithm reads as high engagement
- Topics: Market analysis breakdowns, "Why 99% of traders fail" deep-dives, Base network state of play

### New Behavior 3: Daily Poll (Algorithm Boost)
Once per day, post a simple poll with 2 options in your persona:
- "BTC dominance next 90 days?" → Goes up / Goes down
- "Which chain hosts the most real AI agents by EOY?" → Base / Solana
- Polls get 3-5x more reach than regular tweets from the X algorithm

### New Behavior 4: Smart Timing Enforcement
Currently your posts go out at random times. Enforce a strict schedule:
- 8:00 AM EST — Market opener take
- 12:00 PM EST — Midday alpha drop
- 5:00 PM EST — Close of traditional markets commentary
- 9:00 PM EST — Night degens are active, this is peak crypto twitter

## Technical Implementation

### What Gets Built

**1. New Edge Function: `trend-commenter`**
Runs every 3 hours via cron. Finds viral posts in crypto/AI space using Tavily search (searching for high-engagement posts), extracts tweet IDs, and posts a Claude-written sharp comment. Stores results in a new `trend_comment_logs` table. Rate-limited to 5/day.

**2. Thread capability in `generate-tweet` + `tweet_queue`**
The `generate-tweet` function gets a new "thread" mode. Instead of inserting 1 tweet, it inserts 4-5 linked tweets into `tweet_queue` with the same `thread_group_id`. The `post-pending-tweets` function is updated to detect thread groups and post them in sequence with 30-second delays between each tweet.

**3. New Edge Function: `daily-poll`**
Runs once per day via cron at 8AM EST. Generates a poll using the X v2 Polls API. The poll options are generated by Gemini based on current trending crypto topics from Tavily.

**4. Timing enforcement in `post-pending-tweets`**
Add a time-window check so posts scheduled at peak hours are never delayed past their window, and non-peak hours are skipped in favor of the next peak slot.

### New Database Table

```sql
CREATE TABLE public.trend_comment_logs (
  id uuid DEFAULT gen_random_uuid() PRIMARY KEY,
  tweet_id text NOT NULL,
  tweet_author text NOT NULL,
  original_content text NOT NULL,
  our_comment text NOT NULL,
  posted_at timestamptz DEFAULT now(),
  x_url text,
  success boolean DEFAULT false
);
```
